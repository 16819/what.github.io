Presenting an Electric Motorcycle to a Differently Abled Student

Whilst understanding the essentiality of an electric motorcycle for a differently abled student that can facilitate to work with confidence, the Sociological Association and the Department of Sociology have presented an electric motorcycle to a student in University of Ruhuna. This was presented to the student by the Vice Chancellor on the 5th August 2021.

The initial arrangements to present this electronic motorcycle was facilitated by the Dr. Suranjith Gunasekara, Head of the Department with financial support of a group of friends in Norway and the UK and some of the lecturers in the Department of Sociology.

The Vice-Chancellor of the University of Ruhuna, Senior Prof. Sujeewa Amarasena, Deputy Vice-Chancellor Prof. Saman Chandana Ediriweera and the Head of the Department of Sociology, Dr. Suranjith Gunasekara and a number of academic and non-academic staff members took part in the event arrangement to hand over this present to the student.
